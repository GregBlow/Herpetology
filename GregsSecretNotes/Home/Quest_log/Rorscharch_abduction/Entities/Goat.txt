====== Goat ======
Created Thursday 06 June 2019

AC: 10
HP: 4 (1d8)
Speed: 40 ft.
STR: 12 (+1), DEX: 10 (0), CON: 11 (0), INT: 2(-4), WIS: 10 (0), CHA: 5 (-3)

Senses: passive perception 10
CR: 0

Charge: If the goat moves at least 20 ft. straight toward a target and then hits it with a ram attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 2 (1d4) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a creatgure, it must succeed on a DC 10 STR saving throw or by knocked prone.

Sure-footed: The goat has advantage on STR and DEX saving throws made against effects that would knock it prone.

Actions:

Ram: Melee weapon attack, +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1d4 + 1 bludgeoning

====== Diplomatic tactics ======
Created Tuesday 22 October 2019

With the failure of WMDs (representing one Najaran faction of Magi scientists) to win by force, another faction of merchant spies is given license to win through a strategy of trade and diplomacy, cultivating pliable tribal leaders to conquer the region on the model of the British conquest of India.
